## Accessibility Support

**We are committed to making QSXZ accessible to everyone.** If you have questions or need assistance regarding accessibility, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at [contact@qsxz.ch](mailto:contact@qsxz.ch).

We can help with:
- **Seating arrangements** - Need a chair or rest area during the event
- **Venue accessibility** - Questions about wheelchair access, elevators, or accessible facilities at our venues
- **Location accessibility** - Information about accessibility at hotels, restaurants, and other locations
- **Service animals** - Coordinating accommodations for service animals
- **Dietary needs and allergies** - Meal planning for catered events
- **Other accessibility concerns** - Any other needs or questions you may have

Please reach out as early as possible so we can work together to ensure you have a great experience at QSXZ.
