# Our Venue: Flow60

The Queer Swing Exchange Zürich will take place at [Flow60](https://www.flow60.ch), a beautiful event space just outside the city of Zurich, easily reachable by public transportation.

## Why Flow60?

After an extensive search, we chose this venue because it offers everything we need for an inclusive, welcoming, and joyful festival:

- **Two distinct rooms** — one perfect for dancing, one ideal as a quiet retreat
- **Terrace with a view** — greenery and fresh air for breaks between dances
- **Calm, inspiring atmosphere** — reflecting the spirit of our event

## Accessibility

Flow60 is fully accessible:

- Elevator access
- Step-free entry throughout
- Multiple restroom options
- Gender-neutral bathroom facilities

## Location

While not in the immediate city center, Flow60 invites the local community to discover a new cultural space, and offers international guests a chance to experience the natural beauty of Zurich's surroundings.

The modular infrastructure, creative charm, and peaceful setting make it a perfect location to bring people together to dance, connect, and celebrate diversity.
